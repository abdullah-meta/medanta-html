Why is there a need for Flying Doctors

We are the first medical institution in India to provide tertiary medical care on-board an aircraft even at the altitude of 30,000 feet in the air. Time saved is a life saved in the golden hour after a medical emergency. It is this belief that has helped us deliver time-saving access to Medanta’s expertise across the globe.

How we transfer patients from Bed to Bed

 

One call to get moving

All calls to our air ambulance service number are picked by 24x7 on call doctors experienced in critical care. The doctor on call assesses the patient condition and need after evaluating case history and evaluating reports and clinical records online.

 

Preparing for evacuation

Based on the assessment of the doctor the appropriate medical team, equipment and aircraft is dispatched to pickup the patient from any location, including remote areas with unmanned runways.

 

Take off

At the Airport we have the lowest activation time (time from receiving a call to take off) of 90 minutes. We also get priority for take off and landing, including a green corridor during an emergency.

 

On-board

During flight we are well-equipped and capable of providing advanced critical care support procedures. There is complete ownership of the treatment provided to the patient by Flying Doctors as every treatment procedure and medicine given to the patient on-board is documented.

 

Airport to hospital

Because of our medical centres at the airport, a patient does not need multiple shiftings or transfers. Once the patient lands, they are shifted to an ambulance that will carry the patient from the airport directly to the hospital without having to stop the treatment procedure.
