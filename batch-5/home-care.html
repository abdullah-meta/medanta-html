Medanta fights Covid

We at Medanta, have been at the forefront of this fight against the COVID-19 pandemic and completely empathize with the state of anxiety for you and your family. As a first step, we recommend you to stay calm and help us guide you through this fight against this disease.

It is important for you to understand that 80% of the COVID patients, show mild symptoms of the disease. They don’t need much hospitalization, and can be managed at home by taking specific precautions and by following their Doctor’s instructions. Medanta provides a medically supervised 15-day comprehensive home care program to manage your health with some help from your care-giver, while ensuring safety of your family.

Medanta Covid Home Care Program

15-Day Comprehensive COVID Home Care Package for Mild & Asymptomatic COVID19 Patients

  • Self-monitoring tool with built-in critical alert mechanism

  • Daily on-call monitoring by Medanta nurse

  • Medanta Doctor review via video consultation on Day 3, 7, 10, 15

  • COVID care kit (inclusions: SPO2 monitor, BP machine, Masks, Gloves, Hand Sanitizers)*

  • Detailed COVID care plan

  • Dietician Consultation

* T&C apply | Home Delivery of Medicines, addl. investigations, consultation chargeable. Covid Kit inclusions may vary as per the package.
