Medanta Years to Life

(men/women)

INR 25,000/-

Our most advanced and comprehensive test

This package includes the entire range of all the check-ups and consultations provided in the Medanta Executive Whole Body Check along with Medanta's state-of-the-art tests such as Coronary CT Anginogram, a non-invasive functional test to diagnose coronary artery disease and Calcium Scoring, a special X-ray test to keep check on the build-up of plaque on the walls of the arteries of the heart

Lab Tests

Lab tests help identify patients with increased risk for a condition or disease before they have symptoms or even realize they may be at risk so that preventive measures can be taken.

Complete Haemogram Blood Group and Antibody screening Blood Sugar Fasting & Post Prandial Glycosylated Hemoglobin Serum Urea Serum Creatinine Serum Uric acid Serum Sodium Serum Potassium

Diagnostic Tests
