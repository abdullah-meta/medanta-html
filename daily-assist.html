Our concierge services are designed to take care of your basic tasks and errands, while you are at the hospital.

We understand that hospitalization can be a stressful experience, not just for the patient but also the family and friends. But we pay attention to the small things that simplify life, while they can focus solely on getting well soon. Medanta has enlisted various service providers to help organize a range of ancillary services for the convenience of patients, caregivers and visitors.

We help you with accommodation & travel

Accommodation

Patrons can find comfortable and safe accommodation in the vicinity of the hospital for their preferred duration. This includes booking of hotels or guest houses.

Staying at Medanta
Guest House & Hotels

Tours and travel

Our travel services desk will help you coordinate bookings and ticketing for domestic and international flights, booking bus tickets and railway tickets and also facilitating 'tatkal' confirmations. Patrons may also make taxi bookings from the travel desk.

Tour packages can be availed including religious or sightseeing packages which are managed by experienced industry professionals with an option of customised packages as per specific needs and demands.

Serving you a variety of healthy food

Food and nourishment

Medanta provides a wide range of dietary offerings for its IPD and International patients. Care is always taken regarding each patient’s diet taking into consideration the doctor’s prescription, allergies, nutritional requirements as well as personal preference.

Download our Menu

Cooking classes

We also provide cooking classes to caregivers to help them understand the benefits of certain foods that will help patients recover and improve their health. These cooking classes are free and provided by trained experts in multiple languages. To know more about this service please contact the help desk by dialing 2727 form any hospital phone.
