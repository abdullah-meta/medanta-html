Visitors

  • What are the visiting hours?

    The visiting hours are between 5:00 pm and 7:00 pm for patients admitted in our in-patient wards. Please note that only one visitor is allowed at any time. For ICU visiting hours please Click here.

  • What are the policies and rules for visitors?

    The following are not permitted in the hospital premises:

    • Eatables from outside
    • Flowers
    • Electrical appliances
    • Combustible items
    • Jewelry and other valuable commodities
    • Smoking/ Intoxicants of any nature
    • Children below 12 years of age are not allowed in in-patient areas
    • Private arms or personal security, other than an authorized police personnel

    The attendants are supposed to vacate the room, immediately, if their patient is shifted elsewhere. The hospital reserves the right to discharge the patient in case any of the above mentioned rules are found to be violated.

  • How can I contact patient?

    IPD patients can be contacted by dialing the room number’s extension, when prompted on helpline number, +91124 4141414.
