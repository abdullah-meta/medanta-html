Your treatment journey at Medanta

 

Have your medical history analyzed

Your request is received and your medical history is analysed by our team of medical experts to advise you on the appropriate treatment solutions.

Have a condition ?

Call us: +91 124 4141414

 

Consult even before making a trip

Before you make travel plans, we schedule an online appointment with the concerned doctor to discuss your medical condition in detail through our telemedicine service

Set up consultation

 

Confirm your dates, we'll sort the rest

On confirming your travel dates, the Medanta team will organize all your appointments, local transport and accommodation/hotel booking. Once you arrive, your appointed International Executive will pick you up from the Airport and provide all the required assistance throughout your stay

Speak with our travel desk

Call us: +91 124 4141414

 

Get assistance from admission to discharge

At the hospital, we assist you all the way, right from the admission process, treatment procedures and recovery period until you are ready for discharge. Following successful treatment, the doctor will advise you on post treatment care and follow-up assistance for future appointments, if required

See admission guide

 

Have all your documentation taken care of

On discharge from the hospital we assist you with all the documentation, compilation of all your medical reports, payment process and finally drop you back to the airport.

 

Follow-up after going home via Telemedicine

Medanta helps you make easy follow ups on your medical condition with your doctor by setting up a video conference with any Medanta Doctor. You can also receive a Second Opinion from a Medanta Specialist on a diagnosis you arent sure of.

Set up a follow up request
