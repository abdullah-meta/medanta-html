About the Institute

Medanta Heart Institute houses the Division of Cardio Thoracic and Vascular Surgery (CTVS) and the Division of Cardiology where an integrated team of cardiac surgeons work in tandem. Backed up with the latest technology including a unique hybrid operating suite, the dedicated team of CTVS doctors have performed a staggering 50,000+ CABG surgeries. The team are experts in off-pump CABG, robotic heart surgery, and minimally invasive valve and coronary heart bypass techniques.

Our unique cardiac emergency response unit

Cardiac cases are often life-threatening and require the quick concerted reaction of specialists to save precious minutes. Our unique heart command centre acts swiftly in response to any patient suffering from a cardiac arrest or any other heart related emergency

  1. Cardiac Emergency
  2. Heart Command Center
  3. Further Treatment & Care

Equipped to treat any kind of cardiac emergency

As soon as a patient enters the Emergency the vitals are taken so as to accurately determine the condition. The pecialized "Chest Pain Unit" of the Heart Command Centre helps identify and stabilize persons with heartburn, who may have a pain in the chest that occurs due to acid regurgitation into the esophagus, and may not need further cardiac intervention.
