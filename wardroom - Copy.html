Wards & Rooms

We have a variety of options for accommodation of patients, relatives and caregivers.

Multi-bed Ward

Madanta has made rooms available with 4, 5 and 6 beds in some departments where patient care and Infection control can be sustained effectively. Steps are taken to maintai patient privacy while making all other amenities such as dedicated wardrodes, television, etc. avaiable.

Twin-sharing Room

This is a ‘same gender’ shared accommodation for two patients with a common washroom. Both the beds are partitioned with a curtain to maintain patient privacy. The twin room is equipped with all the basic necessities for each individual. From television, telephone, wardrobe, amenities to attendant’s stay facility, our twin sharing room ensures that both the patients have their privacy despite of a shared room.

Single Room

Medanta's single room is designed to meet all the basic requirements for a single patient with accommodation for one attendant. These are basic and standardised rooms with all the common benefits and services made available for a comfortable stay of our patient and an attendant.

Other than basic offerings, the room includes:

  • A comfortable couch for the attendant
  • Welcome kit that includes: One bath towel | One face towel | Patient gown
  • Laundry bin to keep used clothes
  • Newspaper service

Single Deluxe Room

Medanta's single deluxe room is designed to give enough space for mobility to the patient and accommodation for one attendant with attached bathing facilities.

  • Welcome kit that includes: One bath towel | One face towel | Patient gown
  • Specialized kit that includes: All the preliminary toiletry consumables

The ambience of the room is enhanced by the french windows that allow abundant natural light. To make you feel more at ease, our single deluxe rooms are additionally equipped with refrigerators, additional seating, and a round table.

Super Deluxe Room

With an area of 500 square feet, Medanta's Super Deluxe rooms are located on the 14th and 15th floors of the hospital. Every super deluxe room has different aesthetics and provides a magnificent view of the city skyline. Additional to the single deluxe room features, the super deluxe rooms are equipped with:

  • Refrigerator (with juices and additional drinks)
  • Complementary fruit basket
  • Daily meals for an attendant
  • Hot beverage service (served 3 times a day)
  • Provision for preparing coffee/tea in the room itself
  • Leather recliner for the comfort of the room resident

Suite

With an area of 600 square feet, Medanta Suite is 2 rooms set accommodation designed to meet the privacy of patient and their attendants. The suite includes 2 separate rooms, one for the patient and the other for their attendants with individual washrooms. One of the eye catching features of Medanta Suite are the ‘French Window’ that allows natural light in the room. This room type is a luxury room set that includes all the facilities offered in super deluxe with additional facilities like:

  • Private reading area with a provision of lamp light
  • A complementary fruit basket
  • Daily in-room meals service for two attendants
  • Personalized butler service to order the food of your choice

Contact our Help Desk to help you with accommodation outside the campus in our Guesthouses and Service Apartments
